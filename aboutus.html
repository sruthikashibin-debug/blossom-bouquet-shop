Our Story

Every petal tells a story. Learn about our journey and passion for flowers.

From a Small Garden to a Boutique Shop

Founded in 2015, we started as a small local garden with a passion for bringing nature's beauty indoors. Over the years, we've grown into a beloved boutique, offering unique and elegant floral arrangements for every occasion.

Our philosophy is simple: Be Brave, Be Kind, Be Authentic. These guiding principles shape everything we do, from our curated arrangements to our personalized customer service.

Meet the Florist, blossom bouquet shop

Hi, I'm shruthi, the founder and lead designer. I fell in love with floristry because of the way flowers can convey emotions and bring joy. My mission is to help you express your feelings through beautiful, bespoke arrangements.
